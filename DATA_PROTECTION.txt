Data Protection & Privacy Policy

This page governs privacy policy for Atlassian marketplace add-on "LaTeX JSMath Wrapper Plugin" developed by Andrey Kostin.

What information do the add-ons need?

Plugin is a wrapper for JS library wich is not use any remote server. So all data is rendered on client site.
Your information, whether public or private, will not be sold, exchanged, transferred, or given to any other
company for any reason whatsoever, without your consent.


How is information protected?

No data is sent outside browser, no data is stored locally.


Does it use cookies?

No, it does not use cookies.
